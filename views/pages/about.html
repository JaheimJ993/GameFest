THE FULL STORY

ABOUT

Founded in 2023, GameFest is dedicated to revolutionizing the gaming landscape in Guyana and beyond. As a premier Esports brand, our mission is to foster a thriving community of gamers by hosting high-quality tournaments throughout the year.At the heart of our efforts is our flagship event, the annual GameFest Convention—a gathering of gamers, developers, and enthusiasts to celebrate the gaming culture with exciting competitions, exhibitions, and networking opportunities. However, GameFest goes beyond just one event. We organize a series of tournaments year-round, giving players of all skill levels a platform to compete, showcase their talents, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Whether you’re a competitive player, a casual fan, or someone interested in the business of gaming, GameFest is your hub for everything Esports. Join us on our journey to elevate gaming in Guyana and take it to the next level!

MISSION

Our mission at GameFest is to build a dynamic and inclusive Esports community in Guyana by providing players with opportunities to showcase their skills, compete in world-class tournaments, and experience the best of gaming culture. We are committed to organizing innovative events that inspire, entertain, and connect gamers across the region, fostering growth and professional development in the gaming industry.
