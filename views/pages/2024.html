GameFest 2024

The First Edition.

GameFest 2024 was the beginning of something special. As the very first edition, it brought together gamers, creators, and fans for a full day of tournaments, showcases, and community fun.

From the energy of the crowd to the passion behind every booth and game, the event laid the foundation for what GameFest could become. It was a bold first step—and just the start of a growing tradition.

PARTNERS

A huge shoutout and heartfelt thank you to all the incredible partners of Gamefest 2024! Your support and contributions played a vital role in bringing the event to life. From powering immersive experiences to helping us build a vibrant community of gamers, developers, and creators, your partnership made a lasting impact. We truly appreciated your commitment and are grateful for the role you played in making Gamefest 2024 a memorable success!

WINNERS

As we look back on GameFest 2024, we proudly celebrate the incredible players who rose to the top. This year’s champions showcased unmatched talent, dedication, and sportsmanship across every arena.
